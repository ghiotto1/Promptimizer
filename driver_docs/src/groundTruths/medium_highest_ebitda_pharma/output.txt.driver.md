# Purpose
The provided content outlines a structured approach to generating a SQL query aimed at identifying pharmaceutical stocks with the highest EBITDA (Earnings Before Interest, Taxes, Depreciation, and Amortization). The steps detail the process of organizing data through subqueries, joining relevant tables (`stockindustries` and `earnings`), and performing necessary calculations and filters to refine the results. The final SQL query is designed to select stock symbols, names, fiscal years, and EBITDA values from the specified dataset, focusing on pharmaceutical stocks and ordering them by EBITDA in descending order, while limiting the results to the top 25 entries. This approach ensures that the query is both comprehensive and efficient, addressing potential errors and optimizing the retrieval of relevant financial data.
