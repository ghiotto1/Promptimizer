# Purpose
The provided content is a structured plan and SQL query for analyzing financial data related to semiconductor stocks. The purpose of this document is to guide the user through the process of identifying which semiconductor stocks had the highest net income for the fiscal year 2020. It outlines a series of steps to construct a SQL query, including generating subqueries, joining tables, performing calculations, and applying filters to ensure accurate and error-free results. The final SQL query is designed to aggregate net income data from the `earnings` table, filter for semiconductor stocks from the `stockindustries` table, and then join these datasets to produce a ranked list of stocks by net income. The query limits the results to the top 10 stocks, ordered by total net income in descending order.
