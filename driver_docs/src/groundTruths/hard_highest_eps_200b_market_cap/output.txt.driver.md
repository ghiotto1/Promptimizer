# Purpose
The provided content is a structured guide and SQL query for retrieving financial data from a database, specifically targeting companies with significant market capitalizations and high earnings per share (EPS). The instructions outline a step-by-step process to construct a SQL query that identifies the top 5 companies with a market cap exceeding $200 billion, ranked by their EPS. The process involves creating subqueries to organize and aggregate data, joining multiple tables to gather comprehensive information, and applying necessary calculations and filters to ensure data accuracy and relevance. The final SQL query, encapsulated in a JSON object, executes these steps by selecting and ordering the required fields from the specified dataset, ensuring the results are limited to the top 5 entries based on EPS.
