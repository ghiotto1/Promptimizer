# Purpose
The provided content is a detailed plan and SQL query for calculating the average revenue of the top 25 biotechnology stocks by market capitalization. The process involves creating a subquery to identify these top stocks by joining the `stockindustries` and `price_data` tables, filtering for biotechnology companies, and obtaining their most recent market cap. The results are then joined with the `earnings` table to acquire the latest revenue data. The query ensures the use of the most recent data by employing aggregation functions like MAX for both market cap and revenue, and it groups by company symbol to prevent duplicate entries. The final SQL query is structured to output the symbol, name, market cap, revenue, and relevant dates, ordered by market cap in descending order, and calculates the average revenue across these top biotechnology stocks.
